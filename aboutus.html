Our Story

Founded in 2022, Cat Home blossomed out of an absolute, unconditional adoration for felines. What initiated as a small, passionate family home endeavor has evolved into a respected, delicate cattery specializing in purebred companions.

Our careful breeding protocols are performed under strictly ethical and safe standards, ensuring structural profiles, sweet temperaments, and clean genetic testing remain our primary focus. We raise every single kitten right in the center of our living space, filling their earliest weeks with human affection and active socialization.

Our Mission

To preserve elite breed standards while safely introducing healthy, beautifully structured, and socialized kittens to loving homes.

Our Vision

To set the absolute benchmark for transparent, clean, and ethical standard family-centered cattery breeding operations in Singapore.

Our Values

  • Feline welfare & health first
  • Honest and clear genetics
  • Lifelong structural support
  • Compassionate environment
